# Localization

Let $A$ be a commutative ring and let $S\subseteq A$ be a multiplicative subset. The localization $S^{-1}A$ is obtained by formally inverting all elements of $S$.

The main example is

\[
A_{\mathfrak p}=(A\setminus \mathfrak p)^{-1}A,
\]

where $\mathfrak p\in \Spec A$.

Geometrically, localization corresponds to looking near a point or on a distinguished open subset.
